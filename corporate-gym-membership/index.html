Boost Team Performance with GymNation Corporate Wellness

Empower your team with unlimited access to world-class gym facilities, expert-led group fitness classes, and comprehensive wellness support—available both on-site and virtually.

From dynamic, team-building fitness events to fully customized corporate wellness strategies, we tailor every program to suit your company’s size, culture, and goals.

As a corporate partner, you’ll also enjoy exclusive discounts, making it easier and more affordable to prioritize your employees’ wellbeing—helping boost morale, improve retention, and drive overall productivity.

Let’s work together to elevate the physical and mental health of your workforce.

Corporate membership FAQs

What is a corporate gym membership?

A corporate gym membership is a wellness partnership between GymNation and your company, giving your employees discounted or fully covered access to our premium gym facilities, group fitness classes, and wellness support—across all GymNation locations in the UAE, KSA and BHR.

What does a corporate member do?

Corporate members are employees who’ve been enrolled in our wellness program through their company. They can access GymNation clubs just like regular members—joining classes, using equipment, and taking part in special team fitness events—all while enjoying corporate-exclusive perks and pricing.

What is a corporate club membership at GymNation?

This is a tailored membership designed for businesses, offering flexible packages for teams of all sizes. It includes full access to GymNation clubs, on-site and virtual wellness programs, fitness assessments, and optional add-ons like nutrition workshops or wellness talks.

What does it mean to be a corporate member with GymNation?

It means you're part of a company that prioritizes your physical and mental wellbeing. You’ll benefit from premium fitness services at a discounted rate while enjoying a strong sense of community and support.

Can you give an example of a corporate wellness membership?

Sure! A tech company with 50 employees signs up for our Wellness Plus package. Each employee receives access to GymNation clubs across the UAE, KSA and BHR, joins group fitness classes, attends monthly virtual wellness check-ins, and participates in quarterly fitness challenges organized by GymNation’s team.
